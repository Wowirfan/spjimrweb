About SPJIMR

Top Business School in India

S P. Jain Institute of Management and Research (SPJIMR) was inaugurated in 1981 by the British Prime Minister, the Late Hon. Margaret Thatcher. SPJIMR is a prestigious business school located in the heart of Mumbai and is acknowledged as a responsible member of the Bhartiya Vidya Bhavan.


It is known for the thousands of accomplished and sensitized participants it has sent out into the world for over 43 years—participants who are committed to making a difference. As a self-reliant institution, SPJIMR seeks operational freedom and encourages an entrepreneurial, agile, and flexible organizational culture by influencing practice and promoting value-based growth
